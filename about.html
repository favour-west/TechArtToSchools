Our Mission

We are a team of passionate young people who are tired of the happenings in our society.We clearly know that the educational system in nigeria is bad and we aim to change that via #TATS

Project TATS(Technology Art To Schools) is a school to school sensitisation where we enlighten students on practical steps they can leverage on technology to improve their studies.

Our main goal is to end tech tech illiteracy in Nigeria.
